# Refund Process

## Introduction

Thahawuru strives to ensure that all customers are satisfied with their API services. However, we understand that there may be circumstances where a refund is necessary. Below is the detailed process for requesting and obtaining a refund.

## Eligibility for Refund

### Refund Conditions:

Refunds are considered under the following conditions:
Duplicate payments made by mistake.
Service activation failure within 7 days of payment due to technical issues on Thahawuru’s end.
Cancellation of services within 30 days of payment, if the API keys have not been used.

### Non-Refundable Conditions:

Refunds will not be granted for the following:
Change of mind after API keys have been issued and used.
Partial usage of services.
Non-compliance with Thahawuru’s terms and conditions.
Refund Request Process

### Submit a Refund Request:

Contact the Thahawuru support team via email at [support email] or through the support portal.
Provide your order details, including the invoice number, payment date, and the reason for the refund request.

### Review and Verification:

The Thahawuru support team will review your request and verify the details provided.
This process may take up to 7 business days.

### Approval or Denial:

If the refund request meets the eligibility criteria, it will be approved, and you will be notified via email.
If the request does not meet the criteria, it will be denied, and an explanation will be provided.

## Refund Processing

### Processing Time:

Approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days from the date of approval.
Refunds will be credited back to the original method of payment used during the transaction.

### Notification:

You will receive an email notification once the refund has been processed.
Additional Information

## Partial Refunds:

In cases where only a portion of the payment needs to be refunded (e.g., for duplicate payments), a partial refund will be issued.

### Disputes:

If you disagree with the decision regarding your refund request, you can escalate the issue by contacting Thahawuru support at thahawurusupport@gmail.com or +94 701987654.

### Record Keeping:

Keep a record of all communication and receipts related to your refund request for future reference.

## Conclusion

Thahawuru is committed to providing a fair and transparent refund process. If you have any questions or need assistance with a refund request, please do not hesitate to contact our support team. We value your satisfaction and will do our best to address your concerns promptly.
