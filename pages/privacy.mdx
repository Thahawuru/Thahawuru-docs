# Privacy Policy

## How Thahawuru Handles User Data

Welcome to Thahawuru's Privacy Policy page. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This document outlines how Thahawuru collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

1. Personal Information:
   When you register for Thahawuru, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and identification documents. This information is necessary to create and maintain your digital identity wallet.

## How We Use Your Data

1. Service Provision:
   We use your personal and usage data to provide and enhance our services, including identity verification, digital wallet management, and customer support.

2. Security and Fraud Prevention:
   Your data helps us maintain the security of our platform, detect and prevent fraud, and ensure compliance with legal obligations.

3. Communication:
   We may use your contact information to send you important updates, newsletters, and promotional materials. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

4. Analytics:
   We analyze usage data to understand user behavior, improve our services, and develop new features.

## Data Protection and Privacy Measures

1. Encryption:
   All personal and sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols to protect it from unauthorized access.

2. Access Control:
   We implement strict access control measures to ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data. This includes multi-factor authentication and role-based access permissions.

3. Data Minimization:
   We only collect and retain data that is necessary for the provision of our services. Unnecessary data is securely deleted or anonymized.

4. Regular Audits:
   Thahawuru conducts regular security audits and assessments to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

5. Compliance:
   We comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations, including GDPR, to ensure your data is handled lawfully and transparently.

## Your Rights

1. Access and Correction:
   You have the right to access and correct your personal information at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

2. Data Portability:
   You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format and transfer it to another service provider.

3. Right to Erasure:
   You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions, such as when it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.

4. Restriction of Processing:
   You have the right to request the restriction of processing your data in certain circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of the data.

5. Right to Object:
   You can object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes or other processing activities based on legitimate interests.

## Third-Party Services

1. Service Providers:
   We may share your data with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not allowed to use your data for any other purpose.

2. Legal Requirements:
   We may disclose your data to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from public authorities, or protect our rights and interests.

## Changes to This Policy

Thahawuru reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and, where appropriate, via email.
