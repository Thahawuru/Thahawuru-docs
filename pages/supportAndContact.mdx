# Support and Contact

## How to Get Support for Thahawuru

At Thahawuru, we are dedicated to providing you with the best possible support. Whether you have questions about our services, need technical assistance, or want to provide feedback, our support team is here to help. Here's how you can get in touch with us:

## Contact Methods

### 1. Email Support:

For general inquiries, technical support, or feedback, you can reach us via email at support@thahawuru.lk. Our support team will respond to your email within 24 hours.

### 2. Phone Support:

If you prefer to speak with a support representative, you can call our support hotline at +94-11-2345678. Our phone support is available Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Sri Lanka Time.

### 3. Live Chat:

For immediate assistance, you can use our live chat feature available on our website. Simply click on the chat icon at the bottom right corner of the screen, and a support representative will assist you in real-time.

### 4. Help Center:

Visit our Help Center at www.thahawuru.lk/help for a comprehensive collection of articles, FAQs, and guides that can help you resolve common issues and learn more about our services.

## Support Services

### 1. Technical Support:

Our technical support team is ready to assist you with any issues related to Thahawuru’s digital wallet, API integration, and other technical aspects. Whether you're facing difficulties with account setup, API calls, or system errors, we are here to help.

### 2. Account Support:

If you have questions about your account, need to update your information, or require assistance with account security, our account support team is available to provide the necessary help.

### 3. API Support:

For developers integrating Thahawuru's API, we offer dedicated support to guide you through the process. Our API support team can assist with obtaining API keys, using TestAPIs, and ensuring a smooth integration for your application.

### 4. Feedback and Suggestions:

We value your feedback and are always looking for ways to improve our services. If you have any suggestions or ideas, please let us know via email or through our website’s feedback form.

### Response Times

Email Support: Response within 24 hours
Phone Support: Immediate assistance during business hours
Live Chat: Real-time assistance during business hours
Help Center: Available 24/7 with instant access to articles and guides

### Escalation Process

If your issue is not resolved to your satisfaction, you can escalate the matter by requesting to speak with a supervisor or manager. Our goal is to ensure that every concern is addressed promptly and effectively.
